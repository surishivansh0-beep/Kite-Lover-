Kite Flying Tips for Families

  • For Kids: Start with simple monokites and supervise closely.
  • Safety First: Use strong manjha, avoid power lines, and fly in open areas.
  • Family Fun: Organize picnics with kite competitions—great for bonding!
  • All Times OK: Fly during safe weather; our products work anytime.
